How to Use

  1. Go to Netflix and start playing a video
  2. Click the Sub Loader extension icon in your toolbar
  3. Enter a subtitle URL or click "Browse File" to upload
  4. Select your preferred language (optional)
  5. Click "Load Subtitles" and enjoy!
